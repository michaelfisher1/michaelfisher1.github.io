The Quester Team

At Quester, we employ many talented people, things, and extradimensional beings, all dedicated to improving your questing experience. This project was started by a small group of like-minded individuals who wanted to make a better world for adventurers everywhere. Today, they serve as the Quester Council, and they are always ready to make sure your experience at Quester is nothing short of extraordinary!

Contact Us Join the Council

Warleader Mok'rar was one of the first to join the Quester team. Tired of the constant raiding and pillaging that he had grown accustomed to, he sought a better way to earn glory and riches. After ascending to chief of his clan, Mok'rar "Wildhammer" has brought in his people to serve many purposes in the Quester community, including security, quality assurance, and repossession and distribution of earned equipment.

Michael Fisher joined the Council after appearing from a sudden dimensional rift from a land he calls Australia. He expressed interest in these magical devices he calls "computers", and apparently back in Australia he was working with them as part of a larger mining industry. Before coming through the rift, he was on track to continue with the company and eventually move in to what he called "Application Development and Support". We do not understand many of the things he says, but he seems to be driven to make Quester a reality and a positive influence in the realm.

Archmage Silas has been teaching the novice mages at the Astra Alaria academy for centuries but was so inspired by the idea of Quester that he joined at its founding as a member of the Quester Council. Silas brings a wealth of knowledge to the team, and his vast library at his Inscrutable Tower has helped the Quester community make great strides in the portal network that allows Quester Associates to move across the realms with ease.
